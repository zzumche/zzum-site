My knowledge.

Art

I've been drawing, painting, and sketching since .. my young years, grad school. Always willing to experiment and try something new in this area: new art style or a new method of creating art or a new medium to create art on. Always challenging myself and always improving. Art is passion!

Design

This was the only logical step in my life: I adore art, I am a visual type and I live on apps and websites: so I MUST be a designer. That itch to make it pixel-perfect, responsive, and user-friendly but not boring and not challenging to use - to walk that thin line between genius and mad person design - I LOVE IT!

Front-end

This is my newest curiosity and project: be awesome in Front-end development. Why? Because I want to know it - HTML, CSS, and JS! It is (was) a challenge and knowing Front-end gives me such a good weapon in the dev-world. And you know what - I am glad I was curious, I really dig Front-end. Interesting hobby.

Content creator

This one is PURE FUN! I don't only create pictures, painting and comics (art related card), I make videos, music mementos, board games, photograph, 3d models and stories. I write, draw, edit... I create. And if I don't know how to make something, dammit - I WILL LEARN IT!!
